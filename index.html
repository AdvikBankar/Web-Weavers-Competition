Digital India: Transforming a Nation, Empowering 1.4 Billion Lives.

Explore the journey towards a digitally empowered society and knowledge economy through innovative initiatives, cutting-edge technology, and inclusive growth strategies.

Digital Public Infrastructure (DPI)

The core digital stack: UPI, Aadhaar, DigiLocker and ONDC.

Emerging Tech & Innovation

High-growth, future-focused areas: IndiaAI mission, Quantum Computing, 6G/Telecom R&D and Startup Ecosystem.

India Semiconductor Mission (ISM)

The strategic, national effort to build a self-reliant electronics supply chain, covering Fabs(Manufacturing) and ATMP(Packaging).

National Infrastructure

Physical and digital connectivity: BharatNet(fiber to villages), Vande Bharat(Railways), BharatMala(Highways), and Data Center expansion.

Policy & Governance

The legislative framework and civil service reforms: Digital India Bill(DIB), Digital Personal Data Protection Act(DPDP Act), and Mission Karmayogi.

Sectoral Digitalization

Digital initiatives specific to key sectors: Ayushman Bharat Digital Mission(Health), DIKSHA(Education), and E-Courts.

Digital India is a flagship programme of the Government of India, launched on July 1, 2015, by Honourable Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi with the vision to transform India into a digitally empowered society and knowledge economy. Digital India has been improving the lives of all citizens through the digital delivery of services, expanding the digital economy and employment opportunities.
